Hi, I'm Pitam Poudel. I was born on June 28, 2001, in the peaceful village of Saipu, Ramechhap. My journey as a software engineer is a story of resilience, self-learning, and a deep passion for technology.

I completed my school education in my hometown in April 2016. At that time, I was unsure about my career path. Following my father's advice, I pursued science at the +2 level. Despite studying hard, I realized it wasn't my true calling and did not pass the exams.

During this time, my father received a laptop as a gift from a friend in Switzerland. It was the first time I had ever used a computer. I had no prior knowledge of IT as a field of study, let alone any plans to learn software development. However, I found myself intrigued by coding.

With limited resources, I turned to online platforms, particularly YouTube, to learn Android development. I spent countless hours teaching myself, and this self-study paid off, providing me with a solid foundation in Android development.

In February 2021, I seized an internship opportunity at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited in New Baneshwor, Kathmandu. My dedication and enthusiasm earned me a place in the team, where I gained valuable industry experience and honed my skills.

Despite my professional success, I felt the need to continue my academic journey. I enrolled in the +2 level education program in March 2021 at the school where I completed my school education, balancing work and studies. I prepared independently for exams and successfully completed my +2 level education.

As my expertise grew, I ascended to the position of Senior Android Developer at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited. My discipline, attention to detail, and innovative mindset distinguished me in the company. I consistently delivered high-quality applications, earning the admiration of clients and users.

Parallel to my academic pursuits, I excelled in my role at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited. I leveraged my skills to work on foreign projects through platforms like Upwork, enhancing my professional profile.

In December 2022, I joined the Bachelor of Computer Application program offered by Tribhuvan University. Despite possessing the necessary skills, I recognized the value of formal certification in advancing my career.

Today, I stand as an accomplished Senior Software Engineer. My journey has been shaped by an unwavering commitment to self-improvement and a passion for technology. My story demonstrates the importance of adaptability, resilience, and the pursuit of one's passions. I am a self-driven learner who embraces challenges, continuously grows, and achieves success in the ever-evolving world of technology.
