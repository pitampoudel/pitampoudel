Pitam Poudel, a talented software engineer was born on 28 June 2001, in the serene village of Saipu, Ramechhap. His journey in the world of technology is a testament to his resilience, self-learning capabilities, and determination to pursue his true passion.

He completed his school education in his hometown in April 2016. He found himself unsure of his field of interest. Following his father's suggestion, he pursued science in his +2 level studies. However, he soon realized that it wasn't his true calling. Despite studying diligently for two years, he did not pass the exams.

Driven by his inherent curiosity and desire to explore his passion for technology, Pitam took matters into his own hands. With limited resources and guidance, He turned to online resources, particularly YouTube, to learn about Android development. Spending countless hours honing his skills and delving into the intricacies of app development, Pitam's self-study efforts paid off.

With a solid foundation in Android development, Pitam decided it was time to apply his knowledge in a professional setting. He sought an internship opportunity and was thrilled to be selected at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited in New Baneshwor, Kathmandu on 9 February 2021. His dedication and enthusiasm led to his selection, and he eagerly immersed himself in real-world software development projects. Working alongside a talented team, Pitam gained valuable industry experience and honed his skills.

Despite his success in the professional realm, Pitam felt the desire to continue his academic journey again. He enrolled in the +2 level education program on March 2021 in the education faculty at the school where he completed his schooling. Balancing work and studies, Pitam independently prepared for exams and successfully passed his +2 level education.

As his expertise grew, Pitam ascended to the position of senior Android Developer at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited. His commitment to discipline, attention to detail, and innovative mindset set him apart in the company. He consistently delivered high-quality applications, delighting clients and users alike.

Parallel to his academic pursuits, Pitam continued to excel in his role at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited. He leveraged his skills and expertise to work on foreign projects through platforms like Upwork, further enhancing his professional profile.

In pursuit of formal certification, Pitam joined the Bachelor of Computer Application program offered by Tribhuvan University in December 2022. Although he already possessed the necessary skills, he recognized the value of obtaining a recognized qualification and the doors it could open in his career.

Throughout his journey, Pitam's unwavering commitment to self-improvement, combined with his passion for technology, has shaped him into the accomplished senior Software Engineer he is today. His story is a testament to the power of perseverance, self-learning, and the pursuit of one's true calling.

Pitam Poudel's journey inspires others, demonstrating the importance of adaptability, resilience, and the pursuit of one's passions. He embodies the qualities of a self-driven learner who embraces challenges, continuously grows, and achieves success in the ever-evolving world of technology.
