Pitam Poudel, born on June 28, 2001, in the tranquil village of Saipu, Ramechhap, is a testament to resilience and self-learning. His journey as a talented software engineer is a compelling narrative of determination and passion for technology.

Pitam completed his school education in his hometown in April 2016. Initially uncertain about his career path, he followed his father's advice to pursue science at the +2 level. Despite his diligent efforts, he realized it wasn't his true calling and did not pass the exams.

During his +2 studies, Pitam's life took an unexpected turn. His father received a laptop as a gift from a friend in Switzerland and allowed Pitam to use it. It was the first time Pitam had ever used a computer. He had no prior knowledge of IT as a field of study, let alone any plans to learn software development. However, he found himself intrigued by coding.

With limited resources, he turned to online platforms, particularly YouTube, to learn Android development. His countless hours of self-study paid off, laying a solid foundation in Android development. He was inspired by seeing others develop mobile apps using Android Studio and earn money from it, which motivated him to start his journey in software development.

In February 2021, Pitam seized an internship opportunity at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited in New Baneshwor, Kathmandu. His dedication and enthusiasm earned him a place in the team, where he gained valuable industry experience and honed his skills.

Despite his professional success, Pitam felt the need to continue his academic journey. He enrolled in the +2 level education program in March 2021 at his alma mater, balancing work and studies. His independent preparation for exams led to his successful completion of the +2 level education.

As Pitam's expertise grew, he ascended to the position of Senior Android Developer at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited. His discipline, attention to detail, and innovative mindset distinguished him in the company. He consistently delivered high-quality applications, earning the admiration of clients and users.

Parallel to his academic pursuits, Pitam excelled in his role at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited. He leveraged his skills to work on foreign projects through platforms like Upwork, enhancing his professional profile.

In December 2022, Pitam joined the Bachelor of Computer Application program offered by Tribhuvan University. Despite possessing the necessary skills, he recognized the value of formal certification in advancing his career.

Today, Pitam stands as an accomplished Senior Software Engineer, his journey shaped by unwavering commitment to self-improvement and a passion for technology. His story inspires others, demonstrating the importance of adaptability, resilience, and the pursuit of one's passions. Pitam embodies the qualities of a self-driven learner who embraces challenges, continuously grows, and achieves success in the ever-evolving world of technology.
