Hi, I'm Pitam Poudel.

I was born on June 28, 2001, in the peaceful village of Saipu, Ramechhap, Nepal. My journey to becoming a software engineer is one of resilience, curiosity, and a passion for technology.

I completed my school education in 2016 in my hometown. At that time, I wasn't sure about my career path and decided to follow my father's advice to study science. However, I soon realized it wasn't the right fit for me. Around the same time, my father received a laptop as a gift, and it sparked my interest in computers and coding. Without any prior knowledge of IT, I began learning Android development through online platforms like YouTube. Despite having limited resources, I taught myself how to code, laying a solid foundation for my future career.

In February 2021, I started an internship at Dynamic Technosoft Private Limited in Kathmandu. My dedication and love for technology quickly earned me a place on the team, where I gained hands-on experience and refined my skills. I continued my academic journey alongside work, completing my +2 level education independently in 2021.

As my skills grew, I was promoted to Senior Android Developer at Dynamic Technosoft. I worked on several key projects, including a school management system used by over 500 schools in Nepal and India. I also expanded my professional experience by taking on foreign projects through platforms like Upwork.

Currently, I am the co-founder of a startup where I handle everything related to technology. I work primarily with Kotlin, Ktor, MongoDB, and Kotlin Multiplatform, always exploring new ways to solve problems and create impactful software. My goal is to make a meaningful contribution to Nepal by helping to digitalize my country and improve people's lives through technology.

In my free time, I enjoy playing local multiplayer games with friends. I believe in staying adaptable, continuously learning, and pursuing my passions in the ever-changing world of technology.
